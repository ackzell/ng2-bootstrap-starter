ng2-bootstrap-starter

Angular2 starter kit featuring Twitter Bootstrap, LESS and a gulp workflow.

Another Angular2 starter kit.

So, I recently needed to start playing around with Angular2. I found Pawel Kozlowski's ng2-play and figured that this was my way to go. As I'm not a big fan (meaning I can't really use it in a way that completely pleases me) of webpack just yet and feel more comfortable with gulp it helped me a lot to get up and running.

But I also needed the styling and the benefits of a grid layout I kind of feel comfortable with: Twitter Bootstrap so I threw that in here.

LESS is also part of this mixture of FrontEnd technologies.

Feel free to fork this project, add your own technologies and start learning Angular2.
